Jalpaiguri

  • Home
  • Jalpaiguri

About the destination:

Located in the dooars of Himalayan foothills is the Gorumara National Park- a landscape of dense forests and grasslands, covering the flat plains of Murti and Raidak River systems. It is home to Indian Rhino population besides a great variety of wild life. The Chapramari Sanctuary, the historical Lataguri Forests, the Mal Bazaar and the Tilabadi Interpretation centers add to the attractions of the place. Place to stay - Lataguri, Murti(Banani), Lataguri Hornbill Nest, Kalipur Eco Village, Dhupjhora Elephant Camp, Rhino Camp, Murti Eco Cottage & Neora Jungle Camp. Welcome to the pristine forests of Gorumara, where the critically endangered big beasts' Rhino and Elephants' roams. Stay at tourist cottage; enjoy the scenic Himalayas in the backdrop of river Murti.

Tourist Activities:

  • Wild life

  • Temple visit

  • Bird watching

  • Tea garden visit

  • Jeep safari
Murti (Banani) Eco-Tourism Centre

Address: Locate on map

Murti Eco-Tourism Centre, Utter Dhupjhora , Murti, P.O - Batabari, PIN-735206.

About the resort:

Murti Banani Eco-Tourism Centre under West Bengal Forest Development Corportion Ltd. Is a picturesque tourist destination located on the banks of the Murti River in the Dooars Region. Murti, as a place, is situated besides the Murti River between Chapramari Forest & Gorumara National Park. The entire Dooars Range of forests can be reached from this Eco-Tourism Centre. The forests have a wide range of Wildlives (Indian Bison, Elephants, Various types of Deer & Birds) are also visible from this Eco-Tourism Centre during the early morning and evening hours.
Facilities :-
1. Murti Banani Eco-Tourism Centre, having 17 Nos. of well equipped rooms, with provision of eco-friendly welcome kit.
2. Tourists are welcome here with traditional khadda and welcome drink.
3. Local cuisine is available here (Special mention Boroli Fish).
4. Folk Dance is arranged on every Thursday of the week (the program remain close every June to September due to rainy season). The programme is arranged on special days i.e. Holi, X-mas day, New Year.

Tourist Activities:

  • Bird watching

  • Wilderness Camping

  • Wild life

  • Tea garden visit

  • Temple visit

  • Walk on the River-beds
How to Reach:

Distance between Siliguri to Murti is around 73 km, 18 Km from New Mal Junction and 8 Km from Chalsa. The nearest Railway Station is New Mal Junction, and the nearest Airport is Bagdogra, which is 1:45 Hrs. apart from Murti ETC.
